Quality Policy

Ullmann Laboratories Private Limited is committed to provide testing and Certification Services to its customers which will be reliable and of quality acceptable to the industry and exceed the expectation of our customers.

To achieve this quality objectives ULPL:

  • Uphold and support the quality policy by management at all levels.
  • Ensure and uphold good laboratory practices with a high standard of testing services that will allow us to meet or exceed the requirements of our customers.
  • Operate all testing in conformance with the laboratory procedures and stated test methods with competence, reliability and efficiency.
  • Implement and maintain documented management system to meet the requirements of ISP/IEC17025.
  • Ensure that all laboratory personnel aur competent, adequately trained, familiarise with the quality documentation and committed to implement the policies and procedures in their work.
  • Comply with, ISO/ IEC and GLP requirements.
  • Carry out the periodic review / audit of staff and related work are undertaken to ensure that standards are maintained and opportunities for continuous improvements.
  • This Quality Policy is Endorsed by.

-- Managing Director.
